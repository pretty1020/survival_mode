# Privacy Policy

**Survival Mode Budget Challenge**

Last updated: March 2025

## 1. Data We Collect

- **Budget and expense data** you enter into the app
- **Purchase status** verified via RevenueCat for premium entitlement
- **Anonymous analytics** (if enabled) to improve the app

## 2. How We Use Data

- To provide budgeting and expense tracking features
- To sync data across devices when Supabase cloud storage is configured
- To restore purchases and verify premium status
- To improve app performance and user experience

## 3. Data Storage

- Data may be stored **locally** on your device (AsyncStorage)
- When Supabase is configured, data may be stored in the cloud for sync
- RevenueCat processes purchase data according to their privacy policy

## 4. Third-Party Services

- **RevenueCat**: In-app purchase management. See [RevenueCat Privacy Policy](https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy)
- **Supabase**: Optional cloud database. See [Supabase Privacy Policy](https://supabase.com/privacy)
- **Google Play / App Store**: Purchase processing. Subject to their respective privacy policies

## 5. Your Rights

- You can delete your local data by uninstalling the app
- For cloud-stored data (Supabase), contact support to request data deletion
- You may request a copy of your data by contacting us

## 6. Data Security

We implement reasonable measures to protect your data. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

## 7. Children

The app is not intended for users under 13 years of age.

## 8. Changes

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance.
